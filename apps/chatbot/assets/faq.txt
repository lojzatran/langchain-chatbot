How does the quantum algae technology work?
Our quantum algae technology works by embedding nanoscale quantum dots—tiny semiconductor particles—directly into the algae cells, which supercharge the photosynthesis process by expanding the usable light spectrum from just visible light to include ultraviolet and infrared wavelengths. This enhancement boosts energy conversion efficiency by over 400%, allowing the algae to generate significantly higher biomass yields in shorter growth cycles compared to natural strains. The quantum dots act like microscopic solar panels, channeling extra photons to the algae’s chloroplasts while remaining non-toxic and biodegradable to prevent any environmental harm. Once harvested, the algae is processed through a proprietary extraction method that separates pure biofuels without chemical solvents, ensuring a clean and scalable output. Ongoing refinements include genetic tweaks for even greater resilience to varying salinity and temperature, making it adaptable for deployment in oceans, deserts, or urban farms.
